About Us

Jiaxing Sanjie Electrical Co., Ltd.

Founded in 2017 with registered capital of RMB 10 million, we specialize in R&D and manufacturing of heater modules, PTC electric heaters, and wire harnesses. We mainly serve the heating appliance industry, guided by the core values of “quality focus, technology innovation, and dedicated service,” delivering safer and more practical products.

Putting customer needs first, we continue to innovate while expanding in heating appliances. We supply quality products to well-known brands, earn trust across the industry, and build a strong reputation in the heater sector.

By 2020: 8,000 m² production area, automated lines, constant-temperature lab, automatic full-inspection lines, annual capacity up to 15 million sets.

Our Goal: Become the most trusted core supplier in the heating appliance industry.

Our Philosophy: “Integrity first, quality foremost, wholehearted service.” With strong technical capability, we ensure products that are safe and reliable.

Our Promise: No shipment without full inspection. Strict production to national standards.

Systems & Compliance

ISO 9001

To standardize production, R&D, and sales, control risk, and improve returns, we obtained ISO 9001 certification in 2016.

We continue to strengthen management, raise product and service satisfaction, pursue zero defects, and keep quality at a high level.

RoHS

The EU RoHS Directive (2003) restricts hazardous substances in electrical and electronic equipment. Since 1 July 2006, six categories of hazardous substances have been banned in products entering the EU market.

REACH SVHC

REACH aims to protect human health and the environment and improve chemical safety and transparency. Chemicals imported into or produced in Europe must go through registration, evaluation, authorization, and restriction so that composition is clearly identified and safety is ensured.
